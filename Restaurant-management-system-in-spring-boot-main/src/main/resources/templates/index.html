About US

Welcome to our restaurant in Phagwara, Punjab! We are proud to offer a dining experience that showcases the best of Punjabi cuisine.

Our menu features a wide variety of dishes that are made using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. Whether you're in the mood for tandoori chicken, butter chicken, or chole bhature, our chefs have perfected the art of Punjabi cooking to deliver authentic flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.
